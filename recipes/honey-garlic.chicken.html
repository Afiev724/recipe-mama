Recipe Details

Ingredients and Measurements

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch (optional for thickening)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 green onions, sliced (for garnish)

Info

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Easy

Instructions

Step 1: Pat the chicken dry and season both sides with salt and pepper.

Step 2: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering.

Step 3: Add the chicken and cook for 6 to 8 minutes per side, until browned and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

Step 4: While the chicken rests, whisk together honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, and grated ginger in a small bowl.

Step 5: Pour the honey-garlic mixture into the hot pan and simmer for 2 to 3 minutes.

Step 6: If desired, mix cornstarch with cold water and stir into the sauce; simmer until glossy.

Step 7: Return the chicken to the pan, coating each piece with sauce and heating through.

Step 8: Finish with sliced green onions and serve immediately.

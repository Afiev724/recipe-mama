Recipe Details

Ingredients and Measurements

For the Lemon Garlic Sauce

  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped

For the Salmon Patties

  • 16 ounces cooked salmon (or one 14.75 oz canned salmon)
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ⅔ cup fine almond flour
  • ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon sea salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2–4 tablespoons neutral oil (for cooking)

Optional for Serving

  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Lemon wedges

Info

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 8 patties

Difficulty: Easy

Instructions

Lemon Garlic Sauce

Step 1: In a small bowl combine mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, lemon juice, chopped garlic, and parsley.

Step 2: Stir until fully combined. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Salmon Patties

Step 3: Flake the cooked salmon into a medium mixing bowl using a fork.

Step 4: Add eggs, chopped onion, almond flour, parsley, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly.

Step 5: Scoop portions of the mixture and roll into balls. Gently press them into patty shapes.

Step 6: Place patties on parchment paper while forming the rest.

Step 7: Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add 1 tablespoon of oil.

Step 8: Place patties in the skillet in a single layer. Cook 5–6 minutes until golden brown.

Step 9: Flip patties and cook another 5 minutes until both sides are crispy and cooked through.

Step 10: Transfer patties to a plate lined with paper towels.

Step 11: Serve salmon patties topped with lemon garlic sauce and garnish with parsley and lemon wedges.
